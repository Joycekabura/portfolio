Drainage and Utility Mapping | Saifee Park, Nairobi

The project invloved conducting detailed topographic survey at Saifee Park, Nairobi, to support the upgrading and expansion of the stormwater and sewer infrastructure. The survey combined UAV-based photogrammetry, ground-based surveys and precise leveling of sewer and stormwater lines to capture invert levels and flow gradients critical for planning.

Workflow

  1. Established ground control points(GCPs) across the project area using GNSS-RTK to ensure accurate georeferencing of aerial imagery.
  2. Performed a detailed topographic survey using total station to collect ground points, breaklines and key infrastructure features.
  3. Conducted precise leveling of sewer and stormwater lines to determine invert levels and flow directions.
  4. Processed UAV imagery in Agisft Metashape to generate a dense point cloud and orthomosaic.
  5. Generated a Digital Elevation Model (DEM) and digitized additional site features from the orthomosaic to capture elements not observed during the ground survey.
  6. Compiled total station data, digitized features, elevation contours in AutoCAD to produce the final topographic drawings.

Tools & Technologies

  • GNSS-RTK
  • Total Station
  • Automatic Level
  • Agisoft Metashape
  • Global Mapper
  • QGIS
  • AutoCAD
