Drainage and Utility Mapping | Saifee Park, Nairobi

The project invloved conducting detailed topographic and drainage survey at Saifee Park, Nairobi, to support the upgrading and expansion of the stormwater and sewer infrastructure. The survey combined aerial photogrammetry, GNSS positioning, and ground-based measurements, followed by precise leveling of sewer and stormwater lines to capture invert levels and flow gradients critical for hydraulic design.

Workflow

  1. CEstablished ground control points(GCPs) across the project area using GNSS-RTK to ensure accurate georeferencing of aerial imagery.
  2. Performed a detailed topographic survey using total station to collect ground points, breaklines and key infrastructure features.
  3. Conducted precise leveling of sewer and stormwater lines to determine invert levels and flow directions.
  4. Processed UAV imagery in Agisft Metashape to generate a dense point cloud and orthomosaic.
  5. Generated a Digital Elevation Model (DEM) and digitized additional site features from the orthomosaic to capture elements not observed during the ground survey.
  6. Compiled total station data, digitized features, elevation contours in AutoCAD to produce the final topographic drawings.

Tools Used

  • GNSS-RTK
  • Total Station
  • Automatic Level
  • Agisoft Metashape
  • Global Mapper
  • QGIS
  • AutoCAD

Outputs

  • High-resolution orthomosaic of project site
  • Dense point cloud and digital elevation model (DEM)
  • Stormwater and sewer utility map.
  • Topographic base drawing
